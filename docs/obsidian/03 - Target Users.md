# Target Users

## Customers

### Diaspora Returnees
- Cameroonian nationals living abroad visiting family or doing business
- High intent, willing to pay for reliability and pre-arrival confirmation
- Locations: UK, France, US, Canada, Germany

### International Travellers
- NGO workers, business executives, conference attendees
- Arriving at [[#Douala]] (DLA) or [[#Yaoundé]] (NSI)
- Often on expense accounts, need verified transport

### Local Professionals
- Yaoundé and Douala residents
- Need a vehicle for a period (days to weeks)
- Want transparent, documented rental experience

## Vendors

### Independent Car Owners
- 1-5 vehicles
- Looking for digital channel beyond word-of-mouth
- Currently rely on WhatsApp groups and personal contacts

### Small Fleet Operators
- 5-20 vehicles
- Local rental businesses without online presence
- Want booking system + verified customers

## Internal

### Karu Admins
- Verify providers
- Moderate listings
- Resolve disputes
- Manage commission payouts

## Cities Served

### Douala
- IATA: **DLA**
- Coordinates: 4.0511° N, 9.7679° E
- Cameroon's economic capital
- Busiest port and arrival point
- Highest concentration of business travellers and diaspora returnees
- Airport: [[#Douala International Airport]]

### Yaoundé
- IATA: **NSI**
- Coordinates: 3.8480° N, 11.5021° E
- Cameroon's political capital
- Government, NGO, and conference hub
- Airport: [[#Nsimalen International Airport]]

### Phase 3 (later)
- Bafoussam, Garoua, other Central African cities

## Value Propositions

| For Customers | For Vendors |
|--------------|-------------|

| Verified providers only | Access to diaspora customers |
| Pay with MTN MoMo / Orange Money | Structured payouts |
| Clear pricing, no surprises | Review system builds reputation |

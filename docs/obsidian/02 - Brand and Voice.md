# Brand and Voice

## Identity

- **Name**: Karu
- **Parent**: Zanix
- **Domain**: getkaru.io
- **Tagline**: "Built for Cameroon."
- **Hero promise**: "No stress, no hidden arrangements, no surprises."

## Voice Principles

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Confident | Arrogant |
| Warm | Chatty |
| Direct | Verbose |
| Local-first | Generic SaaS |
| Plain language | Marketing fluff |

## Tone Examples

✅ **Good**: "Book trusted rental cars before you land."
❌ **Bad**: "Revolutionising the future of mobility through next-gen booking platforms."

✅ **Good**: "Pay with MTN MoMo or Orange Money. No card needed."
❌ **Bad**: "Our innovative payment infrastructure leverages mobile money rails."

## Language

- **British English** throughout (organise, travelling, recognised)
- **Bilingual EN/FR parity** — French content equally complete, never an afterthought
- Local terms used naturally: Douala, Yaoundé, MTN MoMo, FCFA, RCCM, carte grise
- Em dashes (—) replaced with commas or full stops in JSON copy

## Aesthetic

**Luxury-functional.** Premium without being cold. African warmth encoded in every grain and gradient.

- Dark backgrounds always
- Amber as the only accent
- Cream text (never pure white)
- Custom serif typography
- Subtle, purposeful motion

## Visual References

- Cluely's product-forward structure
- Editorial design (think dark luxury magazine)
- Premium feel, not generic SaaS

# Product Overview

## Mission

Karu is the first **trusted, structured car rental marketplace** for Cameroon. It replaces the fragmented WhatsApp-and-luck system of ground transport with a single platform where verified providers get digital visibility and customers get peace of mind, before their feet touch the tarmac.

## The Problem

The car rental market in Cameroon runs on personal contacts, informal networks, and zero guarantees. Arriving travellers — especially diaspora visitors, international business travellers, and tourists — have no reliable way to book a vehicle before landing.

The demand is real and growing. The trust infrastructure does not exist. Karu builds it.

## The Solution

A verified marketplace where:
- All vendors are document-verified ([[90 - Glossary#RCCM|RCCM]], insurance, roadworthiness)
- Pre-arrival booking from anywhere in the world
- Transparent pricing in [[90 - Glossary#XAF|XAF]]
- Mobile money payments ([[90 - Glossary#MTN MoMo|MTN MoMo]], [[90 - Glossary#Orange Money|Orange Money]])
- In-app chat between customers and vendors
- Two-sided review system

## Current Status

**Pre-launch.** Waitlist is live at [getkaru.io](https://getkaru.io).

## Launch Scope

- Cities: [[03 - Target Users#Douala|Douala]] (primary), [[03 - Target Users#Yaoundé|Yaoundé]] (simultaneous)
- Onboard 20-50 verified vendors before public marketing
- Booking model: request-to-book (24-hour vendor approval)
- Payments: MTN MoMo, Orange Money via NotchPay
- Languages: French and English

## Business Model

- 15% platform commission on completed bookings
- 5-10% customer service fee at checkout
- Combined take rate: 20-25%
- Launch commission reduced to 10-12% to onboard first vendors

## Key Metrics (Launch Targets)

- 50+ verified vendor listings
- 500+ pre-registered customer waitlist
- Sub-3s page load on 3G
- 90+ Lighthouse Performance score
- WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
